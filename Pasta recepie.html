Homemade pasta

ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

How to make pasta

Ready to learn how to make pasta?Check out this step-by-step.

  1. First,make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface.
    • Add the remaining to the center and use fork to gently break up the eggs.
    • Try to keep the flour walls intact as best as you can!
  2. Next,use your hands to gently mix the flour.
    • Continue working the dough to bring it together into a shaggy ball.
  3. Then,khead!
    • At the beginning the dough should feel prety dry,but stick with it!
    • It might not feel like it's going to come together,but after 8-10 min of
      kneading,it should become cohesive and smooth.
  4. When the dough comes together,shape it into a ball
    and wrap it into plastic wrap.
    • let the dough rest a room temperaturefor 30 min.
  5. After the dough rests,slice it into 4 pieces.
  6. Use a rolling pin or your hands to gently flatten one into an oval disk.
  7. Then ,run it through the widest setting of your pasta maker.
  8. Next, fold the dough...if you want to.
  9. Then, fold it into half lenthwise to form a rectangle.
  10. Finally,cut and cook the pasta
